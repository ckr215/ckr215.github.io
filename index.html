Pramod Films

Pramod Films is one of India's most prestigious film production houses. With our first film dating back to 1958, our history is closely intertwined with that of Bollywood. We pride ourselves at having produced some memorable classics featuring some of the industry's biggest stars. With a young team at the helm, We are now all geared up for a bold step into the future.

Innovative Films

Pramod Films has partnered with Innovative films headed by Pradeep Chakravorty (The late Pramod Chakravorty's son). 'Deva' would be the first film produced by them and they are looking into producing more films in the regional space.

Currently, Pramod films is lead by the founder's grandson Prateek Chakravorty. After a generation, we are back in reckoning, starting with some avant garde projects in Hindi, Marathi and Bengali. Our latest projects in the works are 'Deva' in Marathi and an untitled project in Tamil.

Pramod Chakravorty
Pramod Chakravorty, the director and producer of the 1966 classic Love in Tokyo, was a prolific visionary filmmaker in India who was among the very successful and prevalent filmmakers of his time especially during the 1960’s-70s. He was a true example of the rags to riches story, where at the age of 20, he came leaving his family in Calcutta, boarded a moving train on impulse and landed in the dream city of Bombay with little money, no place to live, no contacts to refer to and no direction of where life would take him forward.

Destiny had it, after several odd jobs he discovered intrigue in cinema, a relatively new phenomenon at that time and finally found his passion to pursue filmmaking. Slowly but surely with determination, observation, lot of struggle and with the perseverance to win, he lived up to build an empire and his company of now more than 50 years called Pramod Films. He made 17 movies in his career span with a very impressive track record of rendering entertaining Jubilee hits across genres. He also had a knack of spotting talent and many of his discoveries have become industry stalwarts in the future.

Prateek Chakravorty
Film making skipped a generation and the proprietorship of his empire was passed to his grandson Prateek Chakravorty who is also getting his foothold in the industry in steps of his grandfather. In his words, Prateek calls himself the I-PAWD man; i.e. I- Produce, Act, Write & Direct. After completing his MBA in Marketing & International Business, his calling also was towards filmmaking and the business of cinema.

He made his debut in a family comedy (Fam_Com) in 2012 called “From Sydney with Love” where in a quirky unique manner he translated his University experience in Australia and weaved a Fresh off beat love story around it. There after he produced few other fresh Indian movies and an international celebrity chat show called “Born Stylish.” Currently he is working on a pre production on a crossover English film to go on floor soon and also writing and directing a dark, gritty television series for HBO expected to take off in early 2017 along with producing other movies in India.
